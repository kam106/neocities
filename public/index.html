Ahem.

Hi. I'm Kam, and I run a Neocities page as a hobby, because I don't know anything about code but felt like picking it up anyway. I've always been fascinated by the site of having my own website page, playing a lot with copy pasted stuff around 2020, but around 2023, I decided to stop and take my own take at an actual site from scratch. I sat over that idea for the past year, and here we are. An idiot who shares a birthday with Ty Dolla $ign has their own site. While this site isn't the best thing ever made, and it's pretty rough around the edges, I still made something passable, and it's good enough.

Disclaimer about buttons, I may or may not be fully aware of the content on the sites of the buttons I add. While they are not explicit, I do add various site buttons for sites both cringy and splendid, you should take anything I add with a grain of salt and assume it was just a site I found if I haven't mentioned it before.

Yeah, I know, a lot of this site is a huge work in progress, and I'm very sorry about not much of it being done. You're free to leave some feedback on my guestbook, or you can also reach out through a link on my resite if this is a little more personal.

Basically, excuse the rough code, sign my guestbook and check out my accounts. Also, a long to-do list.

If this page is a little intense for you to look at, you could always try the older version of this page, where it's just plain text!
